MAHE Manipal offers scholarships to meritorious Girl students admitted to the following branches of the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program under the GENERAL category at Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT) Manipal campus during Academic Year 2024-25
  • B.Tech Automobile Engg
  • B.Tech Chemical Engg
  • B.Tech Civil Engg
  • B.Tech Industrial Engg
  • B.Tech Mechanical Engg

Scholarship Guidelines:

  • The scholarship involves 30 % of the Course fee payable
  • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee including Refundable Caution Deposit of Rs. 10,000 at the time of seat allotment / before the deadline specified for payment of full first year fee.
  • Candidates admitted will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc.
  • After due verification, the list of students eligible for scholarship will be finalized and MAHE Admissions Office will email the MAHE Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the eligible candidates.
  • The excess fee paid (after applying the scholarship) shall be refunded within 1 month of commencement of 2nd semester, only on submission of duly filled Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the MAHE Manipal Admissions office.
  • The award of scholarship for the subsequent years will be same, as awarded during year 1 provided the student continues his/her studies in any one of the above-mentioned 5 branches.
  • In case of upgradation to any branch other than the above-mentioned 5, the student MUST reimburse the scholarship amount availed during the first year back to MAHE.
  • In case of upgradation from the current (listed above) to any other branch, the student will forfeit the scholarship and fee applicable to the upgraded branch will apply
  • In case of upgradation from any other branch to the branch listed above, the student will be eligible for the scholarship provided the academic performance is 7 CGPA & above, with no backlogs
  • Continuation of scholarship for subsequent years, will be based on academic performance of the student at the University examinations preceding the due date for payment of fee / dues i.e. Minimum CGPA of 7 in a 10-point grading system
  • Scholars, who are promoted to next year with less than 7 CGPA, are NOT eligible for scholarship and MUST pay the full Course Fee applicable for that academic year
  • Scholars, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, are NOT eligible for scholarship and MUST pay the full Course Fee applicable for that academic year
  • Scholarship will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc.
  • In case the scholar leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the total Course fee (as per Prospectus 2024) for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal.
  • A student is eligible to avail only 1 scholarship, at any point in time

MAHE Manipal awards Scholar and Achiever scholarships to International / Indian nationals students admitted to Under Graduate (UG) and Post Graduate (PG) programs under the Foreign / NRI / NRI Sponsored category
Scholarship Guidelines
  • Eligibility Requirements
    • Under Graduate (UG) program: 75 % aggregate or equivalent (as per Selection criteria) in 12th
    • Post Graduate (PG) program: 75 % aggregate or equivalent in Under Graduate (UG)
  • Scholarship is applicable for all students who meet the eligibility requirements for the entire program duration, starting 1st year of the program
    • Scholarcategory Scholarships for all students who meet the above eligibility requirements and is applicable for the entire program duration, starting 1st year of the program
    • AchieverScholarship is applicable at the beginning of 2nd year, 3rd year, 4th year and 5th year based on the annual academic performancein the immediate previous year i.e. 1st year, 2nd year, 3rd year and 4th year depending on the Program duration.
  • The scholarship is determined as below
  • Scholarships will be offered to the 15 candidates with topmost MET rank who complete the Admission formalities in the first round of Admission process
  • Minimum Eligibility Requirements
    • 80 % in Physics + Chemistry + Biology taken together, in 12th from a recognised Board
    • Scholarship is applicable for the entire program duration, starting 1st year of the program
  • The scholarship involves 50 % waiver of the Course fee
    • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee including refundable Caution Deposit of Rs. 10,000 before the deadline specified for payment of full first year fee.
    • The Candidates admitted under this category will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc
  • Scholarships will be offered to the 15 candidates with topmost MET rank who complete the Admission formalities in the first round of Admission process
    • After due verification, the list of students eligible for the scholarship will be finalized and MAHE Admissions Office will email the MAHE Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the eligible candidates
    • The excess fee paid (after applying the scholarship) shall be refunded by within 2 months of class commencement, only on submission of duly filled Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the MAHE Manipal Admissions office.
  • The award of scholarship for the subsequent years will be same as given at the time of admission in the first year.
  • Continuation of scholarship for subsequent years, will be based on academic performance of the students at the University examinations preceding the due date for payment of fee / dues i.e. Minimum CGPA of 7.5 in a 10-point grading system
    • Scholars, who are promoted to next year with less than 7.5 CGPA, are NOT eligible for scholarship and MUST pay the full Course Fee applicable for that academic year
    • Scholars, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, are NOT eligible for scholarship and MUST pay the full Course Fee applicable for that academic year
    • Scholarships will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc
  • In case the Scholar leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the full Course fee (as per Prospectus 2024) for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal

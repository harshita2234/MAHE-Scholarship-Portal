MAHE Manipal awards “Kalam – Pai”, “Freeship” and “Scholar” scholarship for the following Under Graduate (UG) programs
  • Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) at Kasturba Medical College (KMC) Manipal, Kasturba Medical College (KMC) Mangalore and Manipal TATA Medical College (MTMC) Jamshedpur
  • Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) at Manipal College of Dental Sciences (MCODS) Manipal & Manipal College of Dental Sciences (MCODS) Mangalore

        This scholarship involves 100 % waiver of Course fee and Hostel fee.
      • However, Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee including Refundable Caution Deposit of Rs. 10,000 at the time of Reporting / completing the Admission formalities.
      • The Kalam – Pai scholar will have to accept the Hostel allocation (Double attached non-AC) by MAHE Manipal. In case the Kalam – Pai scholar opts for upgradation of hostel (if available), he / she is required to pay the differential hostel fee.
      • The Kalam – Pai scholar MUST take care of other expenses like food, books and other academic material requirements.
    • Rs. 5,000 per month will be given to the Kalam – Pai scholar as sustenance stipend, at the end of each month, for the entire regular duration of the program on campus (excluding the internship period).
        • The scholarship will be offered to the candidate with topmost NEET All India Rank meeting the above-mentioned criteria, who complete the Reporting & Admission formalities in the first round of DGHS / MCC Counseling
      • In case of Upgradation / Resignation by this Candidate (as per MCC Guidelines), this scholarship will be awarded to the round 1 admitted candidate with next best rank (meeting the above-mentioned criteria) in the same Institution
      • In case of scholarship vacancy after considering the admitted student of round 1, scholarship will be awarded to the candidate based on NEET All India Rank, after completion of all rounds of counselling.
      • Candidates admitted through Government category (KEA Counseling, JCECEB Counseling) are NOT eligible for this scholarship
    • After due verification, the list of students eligible for Kalam - Pai scholarship will be finalised within 1 month after the completion of all rounds of Counseling.
    • The award of scholarship for the subsequent years will be same, as given at the time of admission in the first year.
    • As the Institute / University does NOT have the provision to fill the vacant seat after the last date applicable for resignation as per DGHS / MCC timeline, if the Kalam - Pai scholar resigns / withdraws from the course, he / she MUST reimburse the total Course fee (all fee instalments) applicable for entire duration as per Prospectus 2024 + Hostel fee + Sustenance stipend, received for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal, before leaving the program

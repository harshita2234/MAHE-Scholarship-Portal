All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi awards scholarships for deserving & meritorious students.

AICTE Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) Scheme for Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) Students

AICTE has introduced the scheme on tuition fee (course fees include tuition and other fees) waiver for economically weaker students, enrolled to the B.Tech program at Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT) Manipal and Bengaluru campuses.

AICTE Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) Guidelines

  • The proposed scheme will be applicable only to the students with parents’ annual income (from all sources) less than Rs. 8 lakhs (or as specified by AICTE) for the Assessment year 2024 - 25
  • AICTE TFW seats will NOT exceed 5% of the General intake for each branch.
  • Candidates with MET 2024 rank will be communicated separately regarding the process of applying for AICTE TFW scheme.
  • At the time of application submission, following documents MUST be submitted
    • Candidate’s parents (Mother & Father) self-attested photocopy of the Income Tax (IT) return form for Assessment Year 2024 - 25 for proof of parents’ income. Once submitted, revised Income Tax (IT) returns will NOT be accepted under ANY circumstances.
      • If the parent(s) are working abroad, they shall submit the following
        • Copy of the 12 month’s salary slip (April 2023 to March 2024)
        • Copy of Passport
        • Copy of Employment Visa
  • After due verification, the list of students eligible for AICTE TFW will be communicated to all the candidates via email for participation in online counseling o On seat allotment in any round, candidates are expected to confirm the allotted seat within the timeline by paying the stipulated fee

AICTE Post Graduate (PG) Scholarships

AICTE awards scholarship for students enrolled in the Master of Technology (M. Tech) and Master of Pharmacy (M. Pharm) programs
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech)
    In pursuance of the policy framework for promotion of Post Graduate (PG) education and research in Engineering & Technology of MHRD, AICTE provides financial assistance to the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) qualified students, enrolled for the M.Tech program offered by Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT) Manipal.
  • Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm)
    In pursuance of the policy framework for promotion of Post Graduate (PG) education and research in Engineering & Technology of MHRD, AICTE provides financial assistance to the Graduate Pharmacy Aptitude Test (GPAT) qualified students, enrolled for the M.Pharm program offered by Manipal College of Pharmaceutical Sciences (MCOPS) Manipal.

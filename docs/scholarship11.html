    Scholarship Guidelines

  • Admission will be done as per the admission criteria mentioned for the respective course
  • The SAGES Scholarship taken together is limited to 5 % of the GENERAL category intake
  • Scholarship is applicable for the entire program duration, starting from the year of admission
  • Minimum Eligibility Requirements
      • Under Graduate (UG) program: 70 % aggregate (as per admission criteria) in 12th
      • Post Graduate (PG) program: 70 % aggregate in Under Graduate (UG)
      • BTech (Lateral Entry) program: 60 % aggregate in Diploma
  • For the Under Graduate (UG) and Post Graduate (PG) Programs with Manipal Entrance Test (MET)
      • MET Rank will be used for the allotment of Scholarship, provided the candidate also satisfies the above mentioned minimum eligibility criteria for the Scholarship. In case the candidate is unable to meet the minimum eligibility criteria, such candidates are eligible to seek admission without SAGES scholarship.

      MBBS at KMC Manipal / KMC Mangalore / MTMC Jamshedpur and BDS at MCODS Manipal / Mangalore

    • There are NO preferential seats for these courses, as seat allotment will be done by MCC / KEA / JCECEB as per MCC / KEA / JCECEB guidelines respectively
    • SAGES Scholarship seats is restricted to
        • 5 seats for KMC Manipal, 5 seats for KMC Mangalore and 3 seats for MTMC Jamshedpur
        • 4 seats for MCODS Manipal and 4 seats for MCODS Mangalore
    • SAGES scholarship involves 75 % waiver of the Course fee payable
    • SAGES Scholarship is NOT applicable for MBBS Admissions through KEA (KMC Mangalore) and JCECEB (MTMCJamshedpur)
    • Scholarships will be offered to the candidate(s) with topmost NEET All India Rank who completes the Reporting & Admission formalities in the first round of DGHS / MCC Counseling.
        • In case of Upgradation / Withdrawal (as per MCC Guidelines) by the Scholar Candidate(s), the scholarship will be awarded to the round 1 admitted candidate with next best rank (meeting the above-mentioned criteria) in the same Institution
        • In case of scholarship(s) vacancy after considering the admitted students of round 1, scholarship(s) will be awarded to the candidate(s) based on NEET All India rank after completion of all rounds of counselling.
        • Candidates admitted through Government category (KEA, JCECEB) counseling are NOT eligible for this scholarship.

      BTech (Lateral Entry) @ MIT Manipal

    • SAGES scholarship is restricted to 5 % of branch intake

      • All other Under Graduate (UG) Programs

    • The SAGES Scholarship taken together is limited to 5 % of the GENERAL category intake

      • SAGES Scholarship

    • The Scholarship involves 75 % waiver of the Course fee payable and is applicable for the entire program duration, starting from the year of admission
        • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee including refundable Caution Deposit of Rs. 10,000 at the time of Admission / Reporting.
        • The scholar will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc
    • After due verification, the list of students eligible for SAGES scholarship will be finalized and MAHE Admissions Office will email the MAHE Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the eligible candidates.
    • The excess fee paid (after applying the scholarship) shall be refunded by within 2 months of class commencement, only on submission of duly filled Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the MAHE Manipal Admissions office.
    • The award of SAGES scholarship for the subsequent years will be same as given at the time of admission
        • Scholarship will be extended in the subsequent years only to those students who have cleared all subjects in the University Examinations of previous year before the due date for payment of annual course fee
        • Students, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, will NOT be eligible for scholarship for that academic year
        • The scholarships will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc
    • In case the SAGES scholar leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the total course fee (as per Prospectus 2024) for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal

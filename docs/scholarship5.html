        • In case of students with the same annual CGPA, the tie will be broken using the following criteria
        • No. of subjects with maximum number of A+ / ≥ 90% of marks

        • Student with highest aggregate marks
        • Student with best aggregate attendance percentage
      • There will be NO scholarship based on the performance in the final year.
        The Achiever scholarship involves 20 % waiver of the course fee
      • The Candidates admitted under Achiever category will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc.
        In addition,
      • Students, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, will NOT be eligible for scholarship for that academic year.
      • The scholarship will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc.
    • In case the Achiever category scholar leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the total Course (as per Prospectus 2024) fee for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal

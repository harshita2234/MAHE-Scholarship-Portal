Manipal Foundation and MAHE Manipal awards Manipal Foundation - MAHE Scholarships to students who have undergone the Scholar Program supported by Manipal Foundation and admitted in the following programs
  • UG Allied Health Sciences
  • B.Sc.Nursing
  • DPharm
  • BPharm

Scholarship Guidelines:

  • This scholarship is open to students from all PU colleges other than those founded/supported /managed by The Academy of General Education (AGE) Manipal / Dr. TMA Pai Foundation institutions.
  • Those students who have attended the full 2 years (11th and 12 grade) “Scholar program” rendered by Manipal Foundation through its partners.
  • Top 25 students in the Karnataka PU Boards examinations based on the Selection criteria i.e. (Physics + Chemistry + Biology) are eligible for this scholarship.
  • The students who wish to avail the scholarship must apply under theGENERALcategory.
  • Admission will be done as per the admission criteria mentioned for the respective program by MAHE.There are NO preferential seats.
  • Scholarship is available for the entire program duration, starting from the year of admission.
  • Minimum Eligibility Requirements
    • For the Under Graduate (UG) and Diploma program: 70 % aggregate (as per admission criteria) in 12th standard Board exam of the Karnataka State Board
    • A certificate from Manipal Foundation stating that the student was a Bonafede student of the Scholar Program for both the 11th and 12th standards and should have a minimum of 75% attendance while pursing the Scholar Program.

MF MAHE Scholarship:

  • The Scholarship involves 75 % waiver of the Course fee payable and is applicable for the entire program duration, starting from the year of admission.
    • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee including refundable Caution Deposit of Rs. 10,000 at the time of Admission / Reporting.
    • The scholar will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc.
  • Manipal Foundation will submit a list of students who have attended the Scholar Program along with other details like marks scored in grades 11 and 12 with special reference to Physics, Chemistry and Biology, attendance tracking in the scholar program and a certificate of attendance from Manipal Foundation.
  • After due verification, the list of students eligible for this scholarship will be finalized and MAHE Admissions Office will email the MAHE Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the eligible candidates marking Manipal Foundation for their information.
  • The excess fee paid (after applying the scholarship) shall be refunded by within 2 months of class commencement, only on submission of duly filled Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the MAHE Manipal Admissions office.
  • The award of MF MAHE scholarship for the subsequent years will be same as given at the time of admission
    • Scholarship will be extended in the subsequent years only to those students who have cleared all subjects in the University Examinations of previous year before the due date for payment of annual course fee.
    • Students, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, willNOT be eligible for scholarship for that academic year.
    • The scholarships will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc.
In case the MF MAHE scholar leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the total course fee (as per Prospectus 2024) for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal

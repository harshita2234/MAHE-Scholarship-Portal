MAHE Manipal offers Freeship, Scholar and Achiever scholarship to meritorious students admitted to the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program at
  • Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT) Manipal
  • Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT) Bengaluru

Scholarship Guidelines:

  • Freeship & Scholar category Scholarships put together, constitutes 5 % of the GENERAL category intake for each branch and is applicable for the entire program duration, starting 1st year of the program.
    • Freeship Scholar
    MET Rank Range Rank 1 – 100 Rank 101 – 5,000
        The Freeship scholarship involves 100 % of the Course fee payable
      • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee including Refundable Caution Deposit of Rs. 10,000 at the time of seat allotment / before the deadline specified for payment of full first year fee.
      • Candidates admitted under Freeship category will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc.
        Scholarships will be offered to the candidate with topmost MET rank in the respective branch who complete the admission formalities in the first round BTech common Counseling
      • In case of withdrawal / upgradation during subsequent rounds, scholarship will be awarded as per merit (MET rank) to Round 1 admitted candidates (same branch / upgraded from other branch) in the same Institution and as per scholarship vacancy after completion of all rounds of counselling (Except Spot Admission).
      • The candidates opting for only waitlisting during 1st round of Counseling are NOT eligible for these scholarships.
    • After due verification, the list of students eligible for Freeship scholarship will be finalized and MAHE Admissions Office will email the MAHE Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the eligible candidates.
    • The excess fee paid (after applying the scholarship) shall be refunded within 1 month of commencement of 2nd semester, only on submission of duly filled Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the MAHE Manipal Admissions office and only to those students who are able to score Minimum 8 CGPA & above, with no backlogs in Semester 1 of BTech program
        • However, Rs. 10,000 would be retained as minimum fee payable in addition to the refundable caution deposit.
      • Freeship scholars are required to pay minimum Rs. 10,000 towards the Course fee every year, for the subsequent years.
    • The award of scholarship for the subsequent years will be same, as awarded during year 1.
        • TContinuation of scholarship for subsequent years, will be based on academic performance of the students at the University examinations preceding the due date for payment of fee / dues i.e. Minimum CGPA of 8 in a 10-point grading system
      • Freeship scholars, who are promoted to next year with less than 8 CGPA, are NOT eligible for scholarship and MUST pay the full Course Fee applicable for that academic year
      • Freeship scholars, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, are NOT eligible for scholarship and MUST pay the full Course Fee applicable for that academic year
      • Scholarship will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc.
    • In case the Freeship scholar leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the total Course fee (as per Prospectus 2024) for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal

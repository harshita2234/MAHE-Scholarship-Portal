(Except for PG Medical / Dental, Super Specialty programs)

MAHE Manipal awards merit scholarships to all students admitted under the GENERAL category, for all under graduate (UG) and post graduate (PG) full time programs, whose father or mother has studied (Full Time) and graduated from the MAHE Manipal Institutions.
Scholarship Guidelines
  • Applicable for Students admitted under GENERAL category only.
  • The candidate is required go through the admission process specified for each program and there are NO preferential / reserved seats for children of MAHE Manipal Alumni.
  • The fee waiver is NOTapplicable to PG Medical / Dental, Super specialty Programs.
  • Eligibility Requirements
    • Under Graduate (UG) program: 80 % aggregate (as per admission criteria) in 12th.
    • Post Graduate (PG) program: 70 % aggregate in Under Graduate (UG).
  • Candidate’s Father / Mother should have studied & graduated in UG program (of minimum 3 years) / PG program (of minimum 2 years) / Super Specialty (of minimum 2 years) / PhD program (including part time) from any one of MAHE Manipal Institutions.
  • The Parent(s) has to register under Alumni portal (https://manipalalumni.manipal.edu/) before submission of the Scholarship application.
  • Details / Documentation Requirements
    • Proof of Registration in the MAHE Alumni portal.
    • Provide Father’s / Mother’s Education qualification details, like Roll No, Program etc at the time of applying, by submitting / uploading all year / semester mark cards OR Degree certificate.
    • Submit / Upload any one of the Government Photo ID proof like Aadhaar, PAN Card, Passport, Driving License of the parent.
  • Scholarship would be provided to maximum of 2 children's only.
Merit Scholarship
  • The fee waiver is 10 % of the course fee.
    • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee + Rs. 10,000 refundable Caution Deposit at the time of Reporting.
    • The candidate will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc.
  • The excess fee paid shall be refunded after the commencement of 2nd semester, only on submission of duly filled Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the MAHE Manipal Admissions office.
  • The award of Fee Waiver for the subsequent years will be same as given at the time of admission in the first year.
  • Scholarship will be extended in the subsequent year only to those students who have cleared all subjects in the University Examinations of previous year before the due date for payment of annual course fee
    • Students, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, will NOT be eligible for fee waiver for that academic year.
    • Fee Waiver will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc.
  • TIn case the candidate leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the total course fee (as per Prospectus 2024) for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal.

MAHE Manipal awards 10 % fee waiver of course fee as scholarship, to candidates admitted to under graduate (UG) and post graduate (PG) programs ONLY, whose mother tongue is Konkani.
Scholarship Guidelines
  • Applicable for Students admitted under GENERAL category only.
  • The candidate is required go through the admission process specified for each program and there are NO preferential/reserved seats for Konkani speaking students.
Konkani Speaking Merit Scholarship
  • The fee waiver is 10 % of the course fee.
    • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee + Rs. 10,000 refundable Caution Deposit at the time of Reporting.
    • The candidate will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc.
  • Candidates must submit the following documents
    • Letter from the passing out School Principal / Institute Director certifying that mother tongue is Konkani
    • Filled up MAHE Scholarship Form 2024 available in the MAHE Manipal Admissions Office
  • The excess amount paid (after applying the scholarship) shall be refunded after the commencement of 2nd semester, only on submission of duly filled Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the Director (Student Affairs) office MAHE.
  • The award of Fee Waiver for the subsequent years will be same as given at the time of admission in the first year.
    • Fee Waiver will be extended in the subsequent year only to those students who have cleared all subjects in the University Examinations of previous year before the due date for payment of annual course fee.
    • Students, who are promoted to next year with failures (F) / Incomplete (I) / Detention (DT) grades, will NOT be eligible for fee waiver for that academic year.
    • The fee waiver will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc.
  • In case the candidate leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the total course fee (as per Prospectus 2024) for the years of scholarship utilized, back to MAHE Manipal.

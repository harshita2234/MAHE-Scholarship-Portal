MAHE Manipal offers interest subsidy on education loan as scholarship, to the eligible students who joined various under graduate (UG) and post graduate (PG) programs offered at MAHE. The scholarship is in the form of providing ‘Interest subsidy’ by partial reimbursement of the interest on education loan availed by them from banks. This scholarship scheme is applicable to the following category of students only
  • Konkani Speaking Students
  • Students who have joined under graduate (UG) Programs (MBBS, BDS, BTech, BPharm) in the Academic Year 2014 – 15 & onwards, and have been awarded Merit-cum-Means scholarship
  • Students who have joined any under graduate (UG) program under the SAGES scheme in the Academic Year 2014 – 15 & onwards
  • Employee’s children and / spouse who have joined various UG & PG programs in the Academic Year 2014 – 15 & onwards, and who have availed scholarship from Manipal Foundation

Eligibility Guidelines

• A student can avail the benefit of scholarship under the Scheme during the term of his / her study.
• Loan shall be availed from a nationalized / scheduled bank for payment of course fee prescribed for the course. Loan availed for payment of repeat term (casual term) fee will NOT qualify for the scheme.
• The benefits under the Scheme will NOT be extended to students in instances of irregularity in attendance, poor academic performance, proven charges of indiscipline and failure in the examination.
• MAHE reserves the right to prescribe the eligibility norms of academic performance for each course.
• The eligible loan for interest subsidy shall be the amount equivalent to the accumulated course fee already paid by the student to MAHE for the respective academic years.
• The fee shall be reduced by the subsidies (if any) provided by MAHE in the form of fee concessions as per Table below:
